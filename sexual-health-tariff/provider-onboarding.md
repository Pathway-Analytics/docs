# Provider Onboarding

## Getting Started

The Sexual Health Tariff Grouper automates tariff calculations for your submitted healthcare activity data. As a provider, you upload activity data, and the system applies the tariff configuration set by your commissioner to generate costed outputs and reports.

Before you begin, you will need:

- A subscription to the Tariff Grouper (Basic Provider or Provider+)
- Login credentials issued by Pathway Analytics
- An understanding of the data format your commissioner expects (see [Technical Guidance](/sexual-health-tariff/technical-guidance))
- Agreement with your commissioner on the tariff configuration that applies to your organisation

## Subscription Setup

To subscribe to the Tariff Grouper:

1. **Contact Pathway Analytics** — Email [enquiries@pathwayanalytics.com](mailto:enquiries@pathwayanalytics.com) to request a subscription. Include your organisation name, the commissioner you work with, and your preferred contact for account management.

2. **Choose your subscription type** — Select the subscription that matches your needs:
   - **Basic Provider** — Data upload and tariff calculation
   - **Provider+** — Includes sandbox testing, KPI reports, and extended support

3. **Receive your credentials** — Once your subscription is confirmed, Pathway Analytics will issue login credentials and provide onboarding support including user guides and contact details for ongoing assistance.

4. **Initial configuration meeting** — A setup meeting is arranged with your commissioner and Pathway Analytics to confirm the tariff configuration, agree data submission schedules, and walk through the application. This ensures everyone is aligned before you submit live data.

For pricing details, see [Subscriptions & Pricing](/sexual-health-tariff/subscriptions).

## Data Submission Workflow

Submitting data to the Tariff Grouper follows a consistent cycle:

1. **Extract activity data from your clinical system** — Pull the relevant sexual health activity records from your patient administration or clinical system for the reporting period agreed with your commissioner.

2. **Format according to specifications** — Structure your data file to match the required format. Field names, data types, and validation rules are documented in the [Technical Guidance](/sexual-health-tariff/technical-guidance). Getting the format right before upload avoids validation errors and resubmissions.

3. **Upload via the Tariff Grouper portal** — Log in and use the data upload function to submit your formatted file. The system accepts the file and begins processing immediately.

4. **Review validation results** — After upload, the system validates your data against the active tariff configuration. If there are errors (missing fields, invalid codes, format issues), the validation report tells you exactly what needs correcting. Fix the issues in your source data and resubmit.

5. **Access processed tariff reports** — Once validation passes, the Tariff Grouper applies the tariff and generates your costed output. Reports are available in the portal for download and review.

For step-by-step instructions with screenshots, see the [How-To Guides](/sexual-health-tariff/how-to-guides).

## Using the Application

### Logging In

Access the Tariff Grouper at [https://secure.pathwayanalytics.com/common/](https://secure.pathwayanalytics.com/common/). Use the credentials provided during your onboarding. If you have forgotten your password or are locked out, contact Pathway Analytics support.

### Navigating the Dashboard

After login, the dashboard provides an overview of your organisation's activity:

- **Recent submissions** — Status of your latest data uploads (processing, validated, errors)
- **Reports** — Quick access to your most recent tariff calculation outputs
- **Notifications** — Alerts about configuration changes, submission deadlines, or system updates

### Uploading Data

The upload function is accessible from the dashboard. Select your data file, confirm the reporting period, and submit. The system provides immediate feedback on file acceptance and begins validation.

### Viewing Results and Reports

Completed tariff calculations are available in the reports section. You can:

- View summary totals by currency and tariff band
- Download detailed line-level costed outputs
- Compare results across reporting periods
- Export data for use in your own systems

### Managing Your Organisation Profile

Your organisation profile contains your contact details, nominated users, and submission preferences. Keep this up to date so that communications from your commissioner and Pathway Analytics reach the right people.

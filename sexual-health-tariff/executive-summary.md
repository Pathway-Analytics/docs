# Executive Summary

> A strategic overview of the Sexual Health Tariff Grouper for senior decision-makers.

## The Business Case

Sexual health services operate under complex tariff arrangements between commissioners and providers. Traditionally, calculating charges from activity data has been a manual, time-consuming process prone to errors, inconsistency, and disputes. Different organisations often interpret the same tariff rules differently, leading to payment disagreements, delayed settlements, and a lack of financial transparency.

The Sexual Health Tariff Grouper eliminates these problems by automating the entire tariff calculation process. It provides a single, authoritative platform where commissioners define tariff configurations and providers submit activity data — producing consistent, auditable charging outputs that all parties can trust.

## Key Benefits

### Automated Tariff Calculation

The Tariff Grouper takes submitted activity data and automatically applies the correct tariff rules to produce accurate charges. This removes the need for manual spreadsheet-based calculations and the errors that accompany them.

### Consistency and Transparency

Every organisation using the Tariff Grouper operates from the same tariff configuration. Charges are calculated using identical rules, ensuring that commissioners and providers always agree on how activity translates to cost. All calculations are fully auditable.

### Reduced Administrative Burden

Commissioners no longer need to manually verify provider invoices against raw activity data. Providers no longer need to maintain complex internal charging spreadsheets. The platform handles the calculation, freeing both parties to focus on service delivery and strategic oversight.

### Standardised Cross-Charging

Where patients access services across organisational boundaries, the Tariff Grouper ensures cross-charging is handled consistently. Charges are attributed correctly based on patient residence and service location, reducing disputes between commissioners.

### Real-Time KPI Reporting and Financial Oversight

The platform provides commissioners and providers with access to key performance indicators and financial reporting in real time. This enables proactive budget management rather than reactive reconciliation at period end.

## Return on Investment

### Time Savings

Manual tariff calculation typically requires significant staff time each month for data processing, charge calculation, and reconciliation. The Tariff Grouper reduces this to minutes — providers upload data and receive results immediately.

### Reduction in Payment Disputes

When all parties work from the same automated calculations, the scope for disagreement narrows substantially. Disputes arising from differing interpretations of tariff rules or manual calculation errors are largely eliminated.

### Improved Data Quality

The platform validates activity data on submission, identifying errors and inconsistencies before they propagate into charging outputs. This drives improvements in data quality at source, benefiting not just tariff calculations but wider service reporting.

### Predictable, Transparent Costs

Subscription pricing is straightforward and predictable. The cost of the service is modest relative to the administrative savings achieved, particularly for commissioners managing multiple provider relationships.

## How It Works

The Tariff Grouper follows a straightforward process:

1. **Commissioner defines the tariff** — The commissioning organisation specifies the tariff configuration, including activity codes, groupings, pricing bands, and any local rules. This is set up once and maintained as needed.

2. **Providers upload activity data** — Provider organisations submit their sexual health activity data through the platform in a standardised format.

3. **Automatic calculation** — The Tariff Grouper applies the tariff configuration to the submitted data, grouping activity and calculating charges according to the defined rules.

4. **Results and reporting** — Commissioners and providers receive consistent charging outputs and KPI reports, enabling informed decision-making and straightforward reconciliation.

The platform is accessed online via subscription, with no software installation required. Onboarding is supported through an initial configuration meeting and ongoing first-line support.

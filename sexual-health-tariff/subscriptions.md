# Subscriptions & Pricing

> Access to the Sexual Health Tariff Grouper is offered by subscription. Each subscription is for a single user and associated with a single organisation.

## Pricing

| Subscription Type | Annual Fee |
|---|---|
| Provider+ | £680+VAT |
| Commissioner | £680+VAT |
| Cluster Manager | £680+VAT + £345+VAT per additional organisation |

## Subscription Period

Subscriptions are offered for a standard period of 10 months. The subscription is automatically extended to 12 months if payment is received within 30 days of invoice.

## Subscription Types

### Basic Provider

- **Target role:** Provider
- **Capabilities:** Data upload, tariff calculation results

A Basic Provider subscription covers the activity delivered by the provider organisation the subscription is associated with.

### Provider+

- **Target role:** Provider
- **Capabilities:** Data upload, tariff calculation, sandbox testing, KPI reports

A Provider+ subscription includes all Basic Provider capabilities plus sandbox (test) calculations, KPI reports, and additional analysis tools.

### Commissioner

- **Target role:** Commissioner
- **Capabilities:** View all provider submissions, KPI reports, configuration oversight

A Commissioner subscription covers the charges raised by residents of the associated organisation and provides oversight of tariff configurations and provider data submissions.

### Cluster Manager

- **Target role:** Commissioner (managing multiple organisations)
- **Capabilities:** All Commissioner features plus multi-organisation coverage

A Cluster Manager subscription is designed for commissioners responsible for multiple Local Authority Districts or Counties. Additional organisations can be added to extend coverage beyond the primary organisation.

## Subscription Transfers

Subscription transfers are available in the event of a new appointee, long-term sickness, or comparable change in personnel. Contact us to arrange a transfer.

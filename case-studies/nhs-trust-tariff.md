# NHS Trust Tariff Implementation

## Client Context

A major NHS trust delivering sexual health services across multiple clinic sites, managing complex tariff arrangements with several commissioning Local Authorities.

## Challenge

Manual tariff calculation processes were consuming significant staff time each month. Spreadsheet-based approaches led to inconsistencies between provider and commissioner calculations, resulting in payment disputes and delayed settlements.

## Approach

Pathway Analytics implemented the Sexual Health Tariff Grouper to automate the trust's tariff calculations. The implementation included configuring the tariff arrangement with participating commissioners, onboarding provider staff, and establishing data submission workflows.

## Outcomes

- Reduced manual processing time by 80%
- Eliminated payment disputes arising from calculation inconsistencies
- Improved payment accuracy across all sexual health services
- Freed staff to focus on service delivery rather than administrative reconciliation

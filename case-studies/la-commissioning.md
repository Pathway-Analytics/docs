# Local Authority Commissioning Transformation

## Client Context

A Local Authority responsible for commissioning sexual health services across its area, working with multiple healthcare providers under varying contractual arrangements.

## Challenge

The commissioning framework lacked standardisation. Different providers operated under inconsistent tariff interpretations, governance processes were informal, and there was no unified approach to monitoring performance or managing configuration changes.

## Approach

Pathway Analytics supported the Local Authority in redesigning their commissioning framework. This included establishing a standardised tariff configuration through the Tariff Grouper, defining formal governance processes, and implementing structured review cycles for ongoing tariff management.

## Outcomes

- Delivered standardised tariff configurations across all participating providers
- Established formal governance processes with clear roles and escalation procedures
- Implemented structured annual review cycles with defined approval workflows
- Reduced administrative overhead for both the commissioner and providers
